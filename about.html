MEET JOE

I was born and raised in New Jersey by a middle-class Italian family, which was centered around food and laughter. I’ve always been inquisitive, intuitive, a risk-taker and curious mind by nature. Anyone who knows me knows I have a healthy imagination. I can be serious or drop dead funny. Most of my career has been in Executive Management for companies such as; Clorox, Oracle, Bristol-Myers Squibb, Price WaterHouse, and a risk taker. I’ve always been a seeker of truth, knowing without knowing that there was much more to life than what meets the eye and finding a life that is worth living. Finding out what I wanted my life to look like rather than just wishing it were different. A life worth living? How do I get that? This is why I wrote the eBook “Don’t Believe Everything You Think”. I wanted to focus on sharing the things I’ve learned that hold us back from living the life we really want. I speak about these things in my book in more detail. I love helping people, so I became a Life Coach 15 + years ago helping people in their personal journey for fulfillment and happiness which eludes so many of us. My coaching training came from the Institute of Professional Empowerment Coaching (iPEC) which is an accredited coach training academy through the International Coach Federation. I share my life with my partner Jill, whom I love dearly, and is all things I have ever dreamed of in a partner. She inspires me to be the “best me” every day. I have 2 sons; Pat and Joe Jr. who have helped me truly understand my ability to give unconditional love. Our two 14-year-old dogs, Brutus (The Pomeranian) and Sweet Pea (The Toy Poodle), who give such unconditional love and emotional support. Every minute of every day
